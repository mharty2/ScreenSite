Pricing Information

Pricing is calculated by a number of factors including quantity, type of screen, type of frame, and installation. Please note that the display price in the info section may not be the actual price. The actual price will be calculated and visible in your cart.

For windows, there is a $2.50 installation fee per window that is capped at $5.00. Feel free to do the installation part yourself, and we'll gladly waive the fee! Additionally, if your order consists of 4 or more windows with the same type of screen, then that group of windows is charged 25% less.

Doors also have the $2.50 installation fee per door, but it is not capped. We will waive the fee if you do the installation yourself. Doors are also not eligible for the group discount that applies to windows.


*For windows and doors that may not fit the "standard size", please use the "non-standard" option when making an order. Estimates are made by size and shape*
